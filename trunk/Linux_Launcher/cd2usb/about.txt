cd2usb 0.5 (for Windows 2000/XP/Vista)

Easily create Live USBs of Ubuntu 8.10+ (also works with Super Ubuntu)

Features:
* Optional persistence (from 128 MB to 4 GB)
* Can use an Ubuntu CD or an ISO (or automatically download one)
* Requires only 800 MB of free space on the USB flash drive
* Does not format the device
* Installs bootloader into device

Homepage and more info:
http://hacktolive.org/cd2usb
